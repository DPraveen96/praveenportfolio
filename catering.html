Work About

Catering Menu & Booking Application

Challenge: Good food has been an essential and mandatory part in events and special occasions. It connects people with their emotions and culture. So there is a need for organised catering crew. To book a catering crew, a person have to travel to visit the store, then they have to look on for menu and select, which consumes more time.

Solution: To reduce the travelling and query time, a catering application will be helpful to see through all the menu and select their desired items and even book a slot on the event date.
